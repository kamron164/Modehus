Who are we

Modehuset Flux, founded in 2002 by Kamronbek, stands as a beacon of fashion in Stockholm. From its inception, Modehuset Flux has curated a unique blend of modern trends and timeless elegance. With a commitment to quality craftsmanship and sustainability, Modehuset Flux has become a go-to destination for fashion-forward individuals seeking curated collections of apparel, accessories, and lifestyle products. As it continues to evolve, Modehuset Flux remains dedicated to empowering individuals to express their unique style and celebrate the artistry of fashion.
